Getting Started with MailThreat Scanner
How to Analyze an Email
  1. Go to the Email Analysis page
  2. Upload a suspicious email file (.eml, .msg) by dragging it to the upload area or clicking to browse
  3. Wait for the analysis to complete (typically 5-10 seconds)
  4. Review the comprehensive threat report to identify potential security risks
Security Features
  • Authentication Checks: SPF, DKIM, and DMARC verification
  • URL Scanning: Detection of malicious links and typosquatting domains
  • Attachment Analysis: Identification of suspicious and malicious files
  • QR Code Detection: Scanning of QR codes in image attachments
  • Audio Analysis: Transcription and analysis of audio files for vishing attempts
Understanding Reports

Each analysis report includes:

  • Email Headers: Information about sender, recipient, and email routing
  • Reputation Analysis: Evaluation of sender email, domain, and IP address
  • Content Analysis: Detection of phishing indicators, suspicious language, and social engineering tactics
  • Detailed Breakdowns: Comprehensive analysis of each threat vector
  • Customizable Reports: Option to generate tailored reports with specific sections
Tips for Best Results
  • Always analyze suspicious emails before interacting with links or attachments
  • Check the Scan History page to review previous analyses
  • For detailed explanations of threat types, visit the Help & Support page
  • Customize your experience in the Settings page
  • For best results, use original email files (.eml or .msg) rather than forwarded messages
